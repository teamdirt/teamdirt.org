Bylaws

Description

Team Dirt follows a set of bylaws, which are meant to provide general guidance for the group. In general, Team Dirt loosely follow the bylaws set forth by IMBA (see, IMBA bylaws), but extend them to include rules specific to our area.

Bylaws

  • Access to Community Events: All events are considered open to the public, regardless of membership status (fees may apply).
  • Team Dirt Board Eligibility: Must be a active member of Team Dirt, with local residence and interest in the Benton County area.
  • Offical Trails: Trails managed by Team Dirt, must meet the approval of their respectful Land Managers.
