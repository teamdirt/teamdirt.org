Description

Bald Hill Natural Area contains a trail system owned and operated by the City of Corvallis. The official trails are primarily graveled, bidirectional, and contain no features. However, the trails are open year round to mountain biking, so they offer a good place to get a quick ride in during the winter months. The park is a great location for beginner riders and those looking for a quick workout near town.

Trails
