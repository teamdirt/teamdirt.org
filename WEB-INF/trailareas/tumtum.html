Description

The Tum Tum Area trail system is primarily focused towards the XC rider. The land is owned and operated by Starker Forests. In order to ride in this area you need to apply for and pick up a free permit from Starker Forests. The permit needs to be displayed on your vehicle dashboard when you park at any of the designated gates.

To get a Starker Forest permit, simply go to their corporate office located at: 7240 SW Philomath Blvd., Corvallis, OR 97330. Monday through Friday from 8am to 4:30pm.

Most of the trails are a combination of logging roads with some single track mixed between. Most notably, the annual MudSlinger race is held here every spring.
