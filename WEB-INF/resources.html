Mountain Bike Organizations (Oregon)

Team Dirt has several sister organizations around Oregon. In addition to sharing build knowledge and trail information, all clubs share a commitment to riding in Oregon and promoting mountain biking. Check out the following list, for information about each club and their upcoming events.

  • Disiples of Dirt (DOD)
  • Greater Oakridge Area Trail Stewards (GOATS)
  • Central Oregon Trail Alliance (COTA)
  • Salem Area Trail Alliance (SATA)
  • Black Rock Mountain Bike Association
  • Northwest Trail Alliance
  • Hood River Area Trail Stewards (HRATS)
  • Rogue Valley Mountain Bike Association

Local Bike Outfitters

Corvallis is host to, no less than, 4 full service bike shops that outfit quality mountain biking mercantile. All of these LBS (local bike shops) offer technical repair and maintenance. Bike brand distribution is pretty well diversified across the shops, so most bike brands are represented.

  • Bike n' Hike
  • Peak Sports
  • Corvallis Cyclery
  • Cyclotopia
  • Coyle Tree Piece, Helmets
  • Bicycle Way of Life

Local Bike Resources
